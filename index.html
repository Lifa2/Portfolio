About Me

I was born and bred in Queenstown and completed my high school education at Maria Louw High School, where I passed my matric in 2018.

After high school, I went on to pursue higher education at Walter Sisulu University from 2019 to 2021, where I graduated with a National Diploma in IT, specializing in Software Development.
